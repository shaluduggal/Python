India on Monday witnessed yet another decrease in its daily coronavirus disease (Covid-19) caseload, with 37,154 new cases 
which took its cumulative tally to 3,08,74,376, according to the Union ministry of health and family welfare’s bulletin at 8am. 
As many as 724 fatalities were reported in the last 24 hours, even with 39,649 people recovering from the virus, the bulletin showed. 

After Monday’s numbers, the death toll and total recoveries in India due to Covid-19 now stands at 408,764 and 30,014,713, respectively.
 The active cases have also climbed down to 450,899, and account for 1.47 per cent of the overall caseload.
